This Privacy Policy explains how Jetpack Battle ("the App") handles information when you use it on Meta Quest devices. The App requests limited access to certain Meta Platform SDK features, including your Oculus/Meta username, in order to provide in-app functionality such as: displaying your name on a leaderboard and identifying you to other players in multiplayer sessions.

No data retention. The App does not collect, store, log, retain, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All information accessed through the Meta Platform SDK is used only transiently, in real time, during your active session, and is never written to persistent storage, a database, a server, or any third-party service.

1. Information We Access

Data typeSourcePurposeRetention
Oculus / Meta username Meta Platform SDK Displaying your name on a leaderboard and identifying you to other players in multiplayer sessions. Not retained — used only in active memory during the session

The App does not access, request, or process any other personal data (such as email address, payment information, contacts, location, biometric data, or device identifiers) beyond what is listed above, unless explicitly stated and consented to within the App itself.

2. No Data Retention

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not store your username or any Platform SDK data on disk, locally or remotely.
  • Does not transmit any accessed data to external servers, analytics platforms, or third parties.
  • Does not create user profiles, accounts, or persistent identifiers tied to your Meta account.
  • Does not use the data for advertising, tracking, or analytics purposes.
  • Discards all accessed data immediately when your session ends or the App is closed.

3. Data Sharing

Because no data is retained or transmitted off-device, the App does not share any user data with third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is retained or transmitted in any case, this applies equally to users of all ages who meet the minimum age requirements set by Meta for Quest devices.

5. Security

While the App does not retain data, reasonable technical measures are used to ensure that any data accessed via the Meta Platform SDK during a session is handled securely in memory and is not exposed to other applications or processes.

6. Your Rights

Since the App does not store any personal data, there is no retained data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about what data is accessed during use of the App, you may contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted at this same URL with a revised effective date.
